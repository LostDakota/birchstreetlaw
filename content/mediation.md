## What is Mediation?

Mediation is a way for two or more people who have a conflict to reach a resolution
outside of court. It is impartial, confidential, and voluntary.

## Does the Mediator Decide My Case?

No. Ms. Zinn's role is to be neutral and help the parties reach their own agreements.

## Is My Case Ready for Mediation?

Parties must be willing and able to negotiate in good faith. All decision-makers must
be present.
