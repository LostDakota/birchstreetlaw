## David P. Sisk — Managing Attorney

**Focus:** Probate · Guardianship · Real Estate · Estate Planning · TEDRA Litigation

Attorney David P. Sisk concentrates his practice on probate, guardianship, real estate,
estate planning, and probate litigation including TEDRA. He previously practiced as a
general litigation attorney before narrowing his focus to areas he is passionate about —
helping elderly and vulnerable individuals and families navigate challenging life transitions.

David is originally from Ohio, where he spent over ten years in state and local government.
He spent two years living in South Korea, where he met his wife Sooyoung. He is a graduate
of Youngstown State University and Rutgers School of Law – Newark.

## Rebekah Zinn — Mediator

**Focus:** Mediation · Dispute Resolution · Family Law · Probate · Civil Litigation

Rebekah Zinn is a retired Washington State Superior Court Commissioner with 18 years of
experience. As a mediator, she helps parties resolve disputes outside of court through a
confidential, impartial process.
